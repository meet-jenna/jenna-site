Frequently asked questions

Do I need to change my phone number?
No. Jenna sits on top of your existing restaurant line. Customers call the same number — Jenna picks up in one ring, 24/7.
Which POS systems does Jenna work with?
Toast, Square, Clover, and TouchBistro out of the box. Orders flow into the same tickets your kitchen already uses — no extra hardware, no parallel system to reconcile.
How does Jenna learn my menu?
Jenna pulls directly from your live POS menu and modifiers. Update a price or 86 a dish, and Jenna knows on the next call — no manual training, no out-of-date specials.
Can Jenna take reservations?
Yes. Jenna connects to OpenTable and Resy to see real-time availability, and confirms the booking with the guest before you ever pick up the phone.
What languages does Jenna speak?
English and Spanish are turned on by default. We also support 30+ other languages, including French, Mandarin, and Portuguese — Jenna detects and switches mid-call.
How long does it take to go live?
Most restaurants are answering calls with Jenna within 48 hours of signing up. We handle the POS connection, port forwarding, and a short calibration call to match your tone of voice.
